My Story

Hello and welcome to my space! As you may have seen on the home tab, my name is Megan and my Github username is @mvperalta. I am a mental health social worker by trade but in 2022, I decided to make a career change into software engineering.

In 2017, I received my bachelor's degree in social work and in 2018, I received my master's degree in social work from Florida Atlantic University, GO OWLS! While my experience in the field of social work has been nothing short of exciting and eventful, I wanted to challenge myself in a different way. What better way than to challenge myself by learning how to code!? Please enjoy my growing portfolio, as I intend to add more projects along the way.
